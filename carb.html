Carbohydrates

Carbs provide glucose to the human body, which gets turned into energy used to perform daily tasks and various functions.
















Why do we need carbohydrates?

The five most important functions of carbohydrates in the human body are:

  • Energy Production
  • Energy Storage
  • Building Macromolecules
  • Sparing Protein
  • Assisting in Lipid Metabolism

Carbohydrates are one of the three nutrients found in foods and beverages. They can easily be found in any healthy diet, but excessively consuming them may cause problems such as high blood sugars.
